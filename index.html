Questions

Things people often ask.

Is the music really AI-generated?

Yes. Every track on Vivi Soul is composed with AI tools, then arranged, mixed, and curated by a human ear so the emotion lands. Think of it as a co-creation — machine + soul.

Is it free to listen?

Always. The channel lives on YouTube and the music itself will never sit behind a paywall. If you want to support, subscribing and sharing means more than money.

How often do new tracks drop?

Roughly one to two new pieces a week, usually late in the evening. Subscribe with the bell on so you don't miss the quieter releases.

Can I use the music in my own videos?

Reach out before commercial use. For personal projects, study sessions, and journaling streams — go ahead, just credit Vivi Soul in the description.
