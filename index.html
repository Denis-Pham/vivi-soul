Questions & trust

The things people ask.

Is the music created with AI?

Yes. Every track is composed with AI tools, then arranged, mixed, and curated by a human ear so the emotion lands. Think of it as a co-creation — the machine helps, the taste is human.

Is listening free?

Always. The music lives on YouTube and will never sit behind a paywall. YouTube itself may show platform ads depending on your account and region. "Free to listen", though, is not the same as "free to reuse".

How often are new tracks released?

New pieces arrive regularly, usually late in the evening. Subscribe with the bell on so the quieter releases still find you.

Can I use the music in my own videos?

Please contact Vivi Soul before reusing music in videos, streams, client work, or commercial projects. Permission and credit requirements may vary by track — and AI assistance does not automatically determine licensing rights.

How do I request commercial use or a collaboration?

Reach out through the channel's About page with a short note about your project. Vivi Soul reads every message and replies to genuine collaboration and licensing requests.
